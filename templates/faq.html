Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of events do you cater to?

We provide catering for weddings, corporate events, birthdays, festivals, private parties, and themed events.

2. What cuisines do you offer?

We specialize in Indian, Mughlai, International, and Street Food cuisines, with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.

3. Do you offer custom menus?

Yes! We allow clients to create custom menus based on their preferences and dietary needs.

4. How far in advance should I book catering?

We recommend booking at least **2-4 weeks** in advance to ensure availability and menu customization.

5. Do you provide decorations along with catering?

Yes, we offer event decorations, table arrangements, and themed setups to match your event.

6. Do you offer tasting sessions before booking?

Yes, we offer **food tasting** sessions upon request to help you finalize the menu.

7. What are your pricing and payment options?

Our pricing depends on menu selection, guest count, and services. We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Bank Transfers.

8. Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes, modifications can be made **up to 7 days** before the event. Cancellations may be subject to charges.

9. Do you offer delivery and live counters?

Yes! We offer **live cooking stations**, on-site catering, and delivery of packed meals.

10. How do I contact you for bookings?

You can call us, send a WhatsApp message, or use our **online booking form**.

About Us

Anjali Caterer & Decorators, located in Bansdroni, Kolkata, has been a trusted name in the catering and decoration industry for over 30 years. Renowned for delivering exceptional service and delectable food, we specialize in making every event memorable with our impeccable attention to detail and creativity.
